About Burke's Bacon Bar

In a search of the perfect small bite, Chef David Burke was inspired to open a brand new restaurant in Chicago that pays homage to one of his favorite ingredients. Enter Burke’s Bacon Bar, a grab-and-go style sandwich shop specializing in palm-sized, bacon-centric sandwiches dubbed “handwiches.” Chef Burke locally sources several varieties of artisanal bacon for his innovative menu in the Near North Side restaurant. Although it’s a key flavor component throughout, he utilizes it in playful, subtle forms like crumbs and jam, meant to please casual bacon fans and die-hards alike.
